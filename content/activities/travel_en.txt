From our location you have 4 main directions you can travel to:
1. North part of Galilee where you will find places like Ein-Tina, the Hahula Valley, and even further river reserves like Snir and Dan. 
2. Western side with Amud river and Meron mountain towards Safed.
3. Southern area with lake beaches and lower Amud River towards Tiberias.
4. East to the Golan Heights towards Quazrin, and eastern lake nature reserves like the Butayha.

There is a lovely park with very nice water pools in the Ancient Quazrin park.
A recommended beach is the beach of Hokok, around 15 min drive.
In the winter period you have the waterfalls in the Golan heights like the Ait waterfall, 
and of course the Hermon reserve to the very north of the Golan.
