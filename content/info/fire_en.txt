The wood for the fire is under the cover.
To light up the fire you can use matches and some paper towels soaked in oil - all located in the kitchen.
You can also find there some tin foil and marshmello sticks.

The grill is available for your use as well and can be lit under the roof outside of the unit, just make sure to bring some charcoal.

Light at the fire pit:
Point the remote on the tree to the projector (no need to take it out of the bag).
1. Press on button to turn it ON.
2. Press the off button to turn it OFF.
3. Change BRIGHTNESS if needed.
