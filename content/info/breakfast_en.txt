Breakfast ingredients are for self-assembly and will be stored in the fridge, including:
sourdough bread, milk, butter, yogurts, granola, jams and cereal. 
You will also find a coffee machine with capsules, black coffee, instant coffee, tea and some cookies.

For cooking and preparing food there are all the basic utensils you need like cutlery, plates, bowls, cups and other equipment.
There is a fridge, a microwave, an induction stove, two types of toasters, a water heater, a pot and a pan for preparing and heating food.
