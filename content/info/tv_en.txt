1. Turn on the TV using the larger remote.
2. Turn on the STREAMER using the smaller remote.
3. CONTROL using the middle circle and buttons around it on the smaller remote .
4. Change VOLUME using the buttons below on the smaller remote.

The TV app is called PARTNER TV - change channels by pressing up and down the circle on the smaller remote.
There is also NETFLIX app available - choose "BnB" from the users.
